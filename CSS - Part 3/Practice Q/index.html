I'm Nobody! Who are you?

I'm Nobody! Who are you? Are you - Nobody - too? Then there's a pair of us! Dont tell! they'd banish us - you know! How dreary - to be - Somebody! How public - like a Frog - To tell your name - the livelong June - To an admiring Bog!
I have been made anew like the first hour of sun after days of rain. I have kissed the face of the life that took from me the only life I knew. Made anew, I have let love render me surrendered, naked as a speck of dust in a beam of day let loose from an opened fist. I have kissed the fist open.
